About Me

Karen J Hernandez is a NYC based freelance photographer who specializes in event and portrait photography.
Photography runs in her blood as her paternal grandfather collected film cameras in his Country. One day,
her father passed on the tradition of photography to her at a young age and ever since that day, she hasn't
been able to put a camera down. Photography has always been the pathway to document stories of those
she encountered and has stuck with that approach with event photography. Whether it be a wedding,
children's events, live music, sometimes portraits for actors or musicians, the end result is to tell a story.
